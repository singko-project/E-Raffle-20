REFERRAL PROGRAM

The Referral Program is designed to reward participants for promoting E-Raffle-20. By inviting others to join, you help grow an engaging raffle community while earning extra benefits for yourself.

ADDITIONAL BONUS RAFFLE ENTRIES
For every successful referral you bring into E-Raffle-20, you receive 1 extra raffle entry.
• These entries are added automatically to your account once the referral joins and is verified.
• There is no limit to how many bonus entries you can earn — the more people you refer, the higher your chances of winning.
• Bonus raffle entries stack on top of your initial raffle entry, giving you an advantage without any additional cost.
• This system ensures that all participants have a fair and transparent opportunity to win.

REFERRAL BONUS
As part of the Referral Program, each successful participant you bring in earns you a ₱10 referral bonus.
• The Referral bonus is automatically processed once the system detects your referral and will be sent directly to your e-wallet.
• There is no limit to how many referral bonuses you can earn — the more participants you invite, the more rewards you accumulate.
• Referral bonuses are separate from bonus raffle entries, giving you both monetary rewards and higher chances of winning in the E-Raffle.
